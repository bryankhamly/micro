## Slide 5: Radiation
- Energy emitted from atomic activities
- Ionizing radiation
  - Shorter wavelength, more energy
  - Can penetrate solid barrier to dislodge electrons from an atom
  - Can penetrate most liquids, and solids
  - X-rays, gamma radiation
- Nonionizing radiation
  - Longer wavelength
  - Radiation excites atoms
  - UV radiation.

## Slide 17: Bactericide vs Bacteriostatic
- Bactericide (biocide, germicide)
  - Agent that causes outright death of microbes
  - Except for endospores
- Bacteriostatic
  - Inhibits growth and multiplication of bacteria
  - Not killed outright; growth might resume.

## Slide 20: Terminology
- Sterilization
  - In the strictest sense,
  - Process that destroys or removes all viable microorganisms
  - Use of heat
- This includes
  - Endospores
  - Viruses
- Generally reserved for inanimate objects
- Commercial sterilization
  - Limited heat treatment used for food
  - Need to destroy *C. botulinum* endospores.

## Slide 22: Halogens and Derivatives
- Group VII non metals
- Chlorine, Iodine
- Very effective disinfectants and antiseptics
- Kills bacteria, endospores, fungi, viruses
- Iodine forms complexes with amino acids and fatty acids
  - impairs protein synthesis
  - Alters cell membranes
- Used for skin disinfection and wounds.
